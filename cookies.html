5. Managing Your Preferences

When you first visit the website, a banner will ask you to choose between "Accept All" (essential + analytics) and "Essential Only." You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser's session storage and refreshing the page.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, blocking essential cookies may affect the functionality of the website.
